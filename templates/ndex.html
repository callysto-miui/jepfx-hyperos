How to use Auto Login

  1. 1. Click "Open Xiaomi Login Page" button
  2. 2. Login with your Xiaomi account in the new tab
  3. 3. After login, you'll be redirected to a callback URL
  4. 4. Copy the entire URL from the address bar
  5. 5. Paste it in the text box and click "Extract Token"
  6. 6. Click "Verify Token" to check account status
  7. 7. Click "Start Unlock Process" and wait
⚠️ Alternative Method:
If auto-extraction fails, manually copy the new_bbs_serviceToken cookie value from browser DevTools (Application → Cookies) and paste it in the Manual Token tab.
